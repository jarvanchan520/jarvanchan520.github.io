How To Buy HTDOGE !

A few steps


  • Install MetaMask or Trust wallet

    For an easy set up, follow their guidline.

  • Connect your wallet to Pancakeswap

    Go to pancakeswap.finance and click the «Exchange» button.
    Sign when Metamask or trust wallet ask you to.

  • Send some BNB to the wallet

    Once you’ve setup Metamask or Trustwallet, you can send some BNB from an exchange like kraken, Coinbase or Binance.
    Or if you prefer you can use your credit card with real money !

  • Swap your BNB for HTDOGE

    Now you can swap your BNB for HTDOGE. Don’t forget to set your slippage at 5-9% !
    Select the amount of BNB you wish to convert in HTDOGE.

  • Click the «swap» button to finalize
